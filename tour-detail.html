Alps Cycling Trip - Tour de France

Ride the legendary climbs of the French Alps

Have you ever dreamt of conquering the most famous climbs of the Tour de France? Join us on this epic trip for an experience of a lifetime. From the chalet base in Morzine, we will guide you up legendary cols, providing full support, pro-level coaching, and an unforgettable team atmosphere.

Itinerary

1

Arrival in Morzine

We will pick you up from Geneva airport and drive you to our premium chalet. You'll meet the team, get your bike set up, and enjoy a welcome dinner where we'll brief you on the epic week ahead.

2

Col de la Colombière

Our first ride takes us up the stunning Col de la Colombière. Enjoy the breathtaking views from the top before a thrilling descent back to the chalet for a recovery massage.

Distance: 95km | Elevation: 1,850m

3

Col de Joux Plane

Today, we tackle the legendary Col de Joux Plane, a challenging climb that has been the scene of many dramatic moments in Tour de France history. The reward is an unforgettable view of Mont Blanc from the summit.
